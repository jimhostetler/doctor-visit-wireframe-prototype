Things to discuss with your doctor

  • Pre-diabetes — example prompts

    • What A1c range counts as prediabetes for me, and how often should we repeat labs?
    • Are my fasting glucose trends concerning, and what target range should I aim for day to day?
    • Would a short trial of home glucose checks (or a CGM) help us see patterns?
    • Which changes—carb timing, activity, sleep, weight—move the needle most for insulin resistance?
    • At what point would you consider medication for prediabetes, and what are the main tradeoffs?
